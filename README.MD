# Tezcatli — Reduce Your Onchain Signal

## 🧠 Elevator Pitch

Most crypto users don’t realize how exposed they are.

Their wallets can be linked to their identity, their assets can be estimated, and their activity can be traced across exchanges and social profiles — all from public data.

**Tezcatli is a confidential exposure intelligence platform that helps users understand and reduce their onchain visibility.**

We start with a simple question:

👉 *“How visible are you, really?”*

---

## 🚀 The Product

### Exposure Scan

Users input a wallet or ENS and receive a **Privacy Score** that shows:

- Identity linkage (ENS, social, entity labels)
- Exchange connections
- Publicly visible assets
- What an external observer can infer

The scan is designed to be **confidential and ephemeral**, minimizing data retention while still delivering actionable insights.

👉 The goal is not just awareness — it’s activation.

---

### Remediation Engine

Once users understand their exposure, Tezcatli provides a **clear path to reduce it**:

- Break identity linkages
- Reduce traceable patterns
- Improve privacy posture

This transforms Tezcatli from a diagnostic tool into an **actionable privacy layer**.

---

## 🎯 Why Now

Onchain transparency has outpaced user awareness.

Tools exist to analyze others —  
but almost no one is helping users understand **their own exposure**.

Tezcatli flips that:

> From “analyze others” → to “protect yourself”
